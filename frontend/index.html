How to Use
1
Create an Account & Login

Register with your name, email, and password. Then log in to access your personal dashboard.

2
Add Study Sessions

Click "Add Session" from the sidebar or dashboard. Enter the subject, your score (0-100), duration in minutes, and any notes.

3
Track Your Dashboard

Your dashboard auto-updates with charts: Weekly Study Hours (bar chart), Performance (doughnut), and Score Trend (line chart). The more sessions you add, the richer the data.

4
Read AI Tips & Recommendations

Go to Tips to Know for personalized study advice based on your weak areas and recent patterns.

5
Use the Study Timer

Go to Study Timer and pick a mode:
• Pomodoro — 25 min focus + 5 min break (4 cycles = long break)
• Countdown — Set a custom duration and study until it's done
• Stopwatch — Open-ended timer to track how long you study
Adjust settings, tag the subject, and your completed sessions are saved automatically.

6
Check Progress & Schedule

Visit Progress for subject-wise breakdown and Schedule for a smart weekly study plan generated from your data.

7
Set Up Your Routine

Go to My Routine and add your busy time blocks (work, school, etc.). Pick the times of day you prefer to study, adjust session length and break time, then hit Generate My Timetable to get a personalized weekly schedule that avoids your busy hours and prioritizes weak subjects.

8
Write in Your Journal

Open My Journal to reflect on your day:
• Pick a mood — Choose from 8 moods (Happy, Confident, Calm, Motivated, Tired, Stressed, Sad, Anxious)
• Get a tip — A personalized tip appears based on how you're feeling
• Write your thoughts — Describe your day, what you learned, or what you're grateful for
• Add tags — Tag your entry (Productive, Learning, Exam Prep, etc.)
• Revisit memories — Browse past entries, filter by mood, and expand any entry to read in full

9
Chat with the AI Tutor

Navigate to AI Tutor from the sidebar:
• Pick a subject — Select the subject you need help with from the dropdown
• Ask anything — Type your doubt, concept question, or study request
• Get instant answers — The AI provides detailed, subject-aware explanations
• Explore topics — Use suggested prompts or type your own to dive deeper

10
Manage Your Settings

Head to Settings to customize your experience:
• Edit Profile — Update your name and email
• Change Password — Keep your account secure with a new password
• Preferences — Toggle Dark Mode and notification settings
• Data Controls — Export all your data as JSON or clear the local cache

11
Meet the Team

Click About Us in the sidebar to meet the five developers who built StudyMate. Hover over each card to see a fun animation and read each member's creative caption!

Quick Tips
  • Add at least 3-5 sessions to get meaningful chart data and AI recommendations.
  • Use different subjects to see the performance doughnut chart fill up.
  • The Pomodoro timer plays a sound when each cycle ends — keep your volume on!
  • Use Dark Mode for late-night study sessions to reduce eye strain.
  • Check your Weak Areas in the right sidebar to prioritize what to study next.
  • Regenerate your Routine timetable whenever your work hours or free time changes.
  • Write a journal entry daily — even 2 sentences helps build self-awareness over time.
  • Check your Journal's Mood Trend chart to spot patterns in how you feel across the week.
  • Use journal tags like "Exam Prep" or "Breakthrough" to quickly find important entries later.
  • Use the AI Tutor when you're stuck — it gives subject-specific answers tailored to your study level.
  • Use the Global Search bar at the top to instantly jump to any page, subject, or feature.
  • Export your data from Settings regularly so you always have a backup of your study history.
  • Visit About Us to see the faces and stories behind the app you're using every day!
